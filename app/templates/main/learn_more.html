Plan Your Finances

It's not about what you make (income), but how much you keep (expenses). Just like a business has its own accounting system, every human being has his own, whether he knows this or not.

The first step to taking back control of your finances is to learn the basics of what an income statement is and what a balance sheet is.

Essentially, what you want to do is have your income purchase things or items that put money in your pocket. These things are referred to as assets. An asset is something that puts money in your pocket.

On the other hand, if you are spending money on things that have no return value, then you risk distablizing your financial situation. These things are referred to as liabilities.
