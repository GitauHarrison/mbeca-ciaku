How To Use The App!

Monthly Budget

  • Press the button "Add Budget Item"
  • Enter the "date", "name" and "amount" of the budget item
  • It is recommended that you provide a very desciptive name in the "Name" field. Ex. Home rent, Tsavo rent
  • Press "Add" to update your budget list
  • You will see your budget displayed once the data is submitted

Income

  • Press the button "Income" tab
  • Press the button "Add Amount of Income"
  • Enter the "date", "name" and "amount" of the source of income
  • It is recommended that you provide a very desciptive name in the "Name" field. Ex. MOH Salary, Safaricom Bonus
  • Provide a "description" to help explain the source of income
  • Press "Add" to update your expense list
  • You will see the details of your sources of income displayed once the data is submitted

Expense

  • Press the button "Expense" tab
  • Press the button "Add Expense"
  • Enter the "date", "name" and "amount" of the expense
  • It is recommended that you provide a very desciptive name in the "Name" field. Ex. Food, Rent, Transport
  • Provide a "description" to help explain the expense
  • Press "Add" to update your expense list
  • You will see the details of your expenses displayed once the data is submitted

Assets

  • Press the button "Assets" tab
  • Press the button "Add Asset"
  • Enter the "date", "name" and "amount" of the assets
  • It is recommended that you provide a very desciptive name in the "Name" field. Ex. Home rent, Tsavo rent
  • Press "Add" to update your assets list
  • You will see your assets displayed once the data is submitted

Liabilities

  • Enter the "date", "name" and "amount" of the liability
  • It is recommended that you provide a very desciptive name in the "Name" field. Ex. Home rent, Tsavo rent
  • Press "Add" to update your liabilities list
